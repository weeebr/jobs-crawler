Job Description

Learning Designer – STEM (80%-100%)


Build the Future of Education

At evulpo, we’re building the future of learning. If you’re an educator, curriculum expert, or learning designer with deep knowledge of the Swiss curriculum and a passion for technology, this is your opportunity to make a real impact.

Why this role matters:

You’ll design high-quality, curriculum-aligned content in Math and Science that helps thousands of students thrive. Working hand-in-hand with product, design, and AI tools, you’ll craft digital learning experiences that are engaging, effective, and scalable.

What you bring:
  • Deep knowledge of the Swiss K12 curriculum Lehrplan21 (Math and/or Science) 
  • Background in education, curriculum design, or learning science
  • Experience working with digital tools, ideally LMS or EdTech platforms
  • Openness to collaborate with AI to generate and refine learning content
  • Clear communication skills and a structured, learner-first mindset
  • Bonus: EdTech or startup experience, multilingualism, or mobile-first content design

What we offer
  • A mission-driven team transforming education with technology and purpose
  • Direct impact on how thousands of students learn every day
  • Collaboration across pedagogy, AI, and product design
  • Flexibility in working style and schedule (hybrid, full-time or 80%)
  • A fast-moving, supportive environment where your expertise shapes the future

Ready to build the next generation of learning?

Send us your CV, a brief motivation note, and (if available) samples of your teaching or content work.
